Some Recipes that I tried
  1. Protein Pancakes - (Preparation time 20 minutes)
    • Preheat pan with very little oil
    • In a bowl add half cup of pancake mix (I prefer Kodiak Flapjacks since they use 100% whole wheat flour which is way better than enriched flour and also have 14g protein per serving)
    • Now add half scoop of whey protein powder (I prefer the OnPerformance brand which provides 30g per scoop)
    • In a separate cup, break open raw egg and add a quarter amount of milk. Mix well
    • Now add the liquid to the flour/protein mix and again mix till there is no dry flour.
    • Don't overstir (lumps are fine). If the mix is too thin consider adding extra flour. If it is too thick add little more milk
    • (optional) Now mash a banana and add it to the mix and stir (Gives good texture and taste)
    • (optional) You can chop strawberries and add to the mix and stir too (Makes pancakes sweet)
    • Now add the mixed batter in small proportions (as desired) to the hot pan
    • Gently flip after 1-2 minutes
    • Once both sides are cooked, put the pancake on a plate with a paper towel(to prevent soggy pancake at bottom)
    • Repeat the process for more pancakes. You don't have to oil the pan after the first pancake.
    • The portions listed above should give you 3-5 pancakes depending on the portion sizes you put in the pan
    • Serve with butter/Maple Syrup/fruit jam
  2. Kale and Egg Salad
    • Boil 2 eggs
    • just dip the chopped kale into the hot water (after boiling the eggs) for 1-2 minutes
    • Drain water from the kale and dice the boiled eggs and mix kale with the eggs
    • Add chopped strawberries, cottage cheese, and chopped dry plums
    • Add sliced olives and mix all the ingredients
    • Eat as is! Can add salt and pepper as desired
