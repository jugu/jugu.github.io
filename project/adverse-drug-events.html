Identification and Attribution of Adverse Drug Events

Background

A patient is readmitted to hospital complaining about certain symptoms.
The patient mentions the medications he/she has been taking as prescribed previously.
The doctor goes through the prescribed medicine and correlates them with the symptoms to determine what caused the side effects.
There can be many symptoms that can be attributed to a drug's side effects. Many drugs may give the same side effect, or a combination of the drugs may lead to the side effect. Sometimes the dosages of medications may also lead to the side effects.
This becomes an ardous task for the doctor to determine which medication(s) is/are the prominent cause of the side effects reported by the patient.
How do we quickly determine which medication is the culprit?
